Arranger

An arranger serves a multitude of roles, helping your vision come to life in many different forms. Whether it's taking your song from guitar/vocals to a full band arrangement, transcribing an old jazz piece for a 20-piece big band ensemble, or enhancing an existing track with the addition of a string quartet, Tim possesses the versatile skills and expertise needed.

Having had experience writing for everything from solo guitar to full scale orchestra, Tim is capable of writing whatever charts are needed and sourcing the best session musicians on his books for the job!
